Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

TabStacks works silently in the background, keeping your tabs organized without interrupting your flow.

Auto-group by domain

Every tab is automatically assigned to a collapsible stack based on its domain. Open ten GitHub tabs? One neat group.

Switcher bar overlay

A lightweight visual bar lets you jump between stacks instantly. No hunting through a horizontal sea of favicons.

Keyboard shortcuts

Navigate stacks without touching your mouse. Works independently of the switcher bar for pure keyboard workflows.

Search across tabs

Quickly find any open tab by title or URL. Stop re-opening tabs you already have.

Theme support

Matches your Chrome theme. Looks sharp whether you're running dark mode or light.

Fully private

No accounts, no telemetry, no network requests. All state is stored locally on your device.
